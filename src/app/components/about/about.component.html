Our Mission

Giving Every Kid
the Right Start

Every year, The Jones Gym opens its doors to the community to give children everything they need to walk into the classroom ready to learn. From backpacks and school supplies to health screenings, haircuts, and live entertainment — the Back to School Bash is 100% free for families.

None of it would be possible without the generous businesses, sponsors, volunteers, and performers who show up for our kids. This is your invitation to be part of something bigger.

How to Participate

Ways You Can Help

🎪

Activity Stations

Bounce houses, face painting, crafts, games, vision screenings, and more. Bring your service to our kids!

🍕

Food & Drinks

Donate or serve food, snacks, or beverages. Help keep the energy up for a full day of fun.

🎒

Supply Donations

Contribute backpacks, school supplies, snacks, ready-to-eat meals, or gift cards for students in need.

🎤

Entertainment

Perform on stage for 30 minutes — musicians, magicians, dance groups, or anything that gets kids moving.

🏆

Sponsorship

From Community Sponsor to Heavyweight ($1,000+), every level comes with recognition and community goodwill.

🙌

Volunteer

Give your time on the day of the event. Setup, coordination, and support roles available for everyone.
